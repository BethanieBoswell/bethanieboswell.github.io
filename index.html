Customer success without the guesswork

See what it’s really like to be a Customer Success Manager—day-to-day tasks, strategies, and skills, explained clearly and applied in real-world scenarios.

Customer success is one of the fastest-growing professions in today’s business world, but breaking into the role can feel unclear from the outside.

Customer Success, Decoded takes the mystery out of the job, giving you a clear picture of what CSMs do, how they do it, and the strategies that make them successful.

Inside, you will:

  • Master the CSM Toolkit with chapters on everything from onboarding and demonstrating ROI to managing renewals and expansion.
  • Learn to Handle Any Challenge with proven frameworks for de-escalating conflict, managing difficult conversations, and navigating churn.
  • Gain a Strategic Edge by learning how to segment accounts, prepare for high-stakes executive business reviews, and align with internal teams.
  • Bridge Theory and Practice using the included adaptable templates, checklists, and playbooks for every stage of the customer journey.

Written by a veteran educator skilled at translating complex, insider knowledge into clear, actionable steps for career changers and newcomers alike, this book is designed to give you both the understanding and the practical tools to succeed in your first CSM role.

About the Author

Bethanie Boswell has spent her career striving to fulfill her personal mission to deliver demonstrable value and positive outcomes in every role. During twelve years as a classroom teacher, she listened actively, diagnosed hidden needs, anticipated newcomers’ questions, and developed creative solutions for unique challenges. At Georgia Public Broadcasting, she designed and operated an innovative program bringing emerging tech to thousands of educators and students. Later, as a professional learning specialist, she traveled nationwide to support a new bilingual early literacy program, equipping teachers with the tools and strategies for a successful rollout.

This focus on building true partnerships where every stakeholder benefits, combined with the realization that a profession existed to blend skills like relationship building, creative problem-solving, project management, and strategic enablement, prompted a deep exploration of the field. The result is Customer Success, Decoded, a product of her focused effort to deconstruct the profession from the valuable perspective of a dedicated newcomer.
